Timeline of President Roosevelts life:

  • 1858 - Born October 27 in New York City.
  • 1876 - Attended Harvard College
  • 1880 - On his 22nd Birthday, married Alice Hathaway Lee. She would die 4 years later, 2 days after giving birth to their first child, Alice Lee.
  • 1882 - New York State Assemblyman until 1884. He immediately began making his mark, specifically in corporate corruption issues.
  • 1884 - Retires from politics and moves to North Dakota to live the "Cowboy life".
  • 1886 - Second marriage to Edith Kermit Carow. They would have 5 children.
  • 1886 - Returns to New York.
  • 1894 - Becomes New York City Police Commissioner, reforming one of the most corrupt police departments of that period.
  • 1897 - Appointed Assistant Secretary of the Navy.
  • 1898 - Resigns as Secretary of the Navy to form the "Rough Riders" and lead them into battle in Cuba during the Spanish-American war.
  • 1898 - After his tour in Cuba, returned home a hero and was subsequntly ask to run for Governor of New York, which he won.
  • 1900 - Elected as Vice President of the United Staes under William McKinley.
  • 1901 - Became 26th president after President McKinley's assassination.
  • 1904 - Elected to full term
  • 1919 - Died January 6 in Oyster Bay, New York
